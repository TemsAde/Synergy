About Synergy Specialty

Executive Summary

Synergy Specialty is a registered corporation in the healthcare industry aimed at engaging in the business of manufacturing, marketing and sales of pharmaceutical and healthcare products. It is a company established by partners with vast knowledge and experience of the healthcare industry.

The company is into technical collaboration with various manufacturers with world class facilities, most of whom are market leaders in their areas of specialization in order to deliver unique products and cost-effective services. It is also desirous of establishing her own manufacturing facility in Nigeria to further enhance her competitive advantage.

With a business model that is centered on qualitative products & services, high ethical standards and continuous improvement, the company is positioned to move towards growth and profitability from inception.

Business Scope

  • Marketing and Sales of pharmaceuticals
  • Importation, Marketing and Sales of Pharmaceutical Raw Materials to local manufacturers.
  • Marketing and Sales of laboratory/ medical equipment, medical devices and hospital consumables
  • Local Manufacturing / Contract-manufacturing of Pharmaceutical products.
  • Export of Science Based Locally derived/ manufactured products.

Competitive Strengths

  • Innovative/ quality products Marketing and Sales of pharmaceuticals
  • Experienced & professional management team
  • Swift and Adaptable Systems.
  • Strategic alliance with local and international companies.

Personnel

Since the quality of an organization's employees directly influences its ability to gain and sustain competitive advantage, the strength of our workforce lies in the synergy of qualitatively trained personnel, guided by professionalism and integrity with the aim of meeting the need of our customers and the society at large

Operations

Strategic Alliances

Amegh Pharma PVT. Ltd

Amegh Pharma was founded in July 2007 as a Private Limited Company, incorporated in India under the Companies Act of 1956. Its founder and CEO is Mr. Vipul Mohan, who is an MBA from the S.P. Jain Institute of Management and Research, Mumbai, India, which has been consistently ranked among the top 10 business schools of India. He has over 16 years experience of International Business, all around the world, gained by his terms at different levels at some of the top large and mid-sized pharmaceutical companies in India like Ranbaxy, Lupin, Ajanta and MJ.

Bhargava Phytolab PVT Ltd

With almost a 100 years of experience in providing healthcare and an unbroken commitment to those who rely on our treatments, India based Bhargava Phytolab has today become one of the world's leading homeopathic company. Homeopathy is based on the simple principle of stimulating the body's own healing response. The company uphold the superiority of Homeopathy through expertise supervision of qualified technical professionals following Hahnemanian method. Today, every 3.6 seconds, someone around the world purchases a Bhargava Phytolab product. Making innovation the touchstone of its existence, Bhargava Phytolab has invested in decades of research and development, production as well as marketing of more than 3000 products (including generics and branded formulations). The company partners with distributors and retail chains to make homeopathic medicines available to a wide variety of consumers and physician and has thus fulfilled its mission of providing quality medicines at affordable prices to all.

Top Gloves

Top Glove is the largest rubber glove manufacturer in the world, producing 20 billion pairs per year and exporting its products to about 170 countries of the world. Top Glove is an ISO 9001 Certified company whose main focus is to be a “One- Stop Complete Glove Sourcing Center”. Realizing the importance of continuously staying ahead in product development, Top Glove has placed a lot of emphasis on Research & Development to produce a wide and diversified range of high quality and value added glove products in order to cater to the ever increasing expectation of End- users.

Himedia

Himedia Laboratories Pvt Ltd is an India based manufacturer of high quality Biosciences Chemicals , Reagents, Laboratory appliances for the Healthcare, Food , Pharmaceutical and Research Industries. The company innovative styles has positioned her as a World Leader with high quality standards. Himedia is represented in Nigeria by Synergy Specialty Ltd.

Miraflash Nigeria

Miraflash is a fully indigenous pharmaceutical manufacturing company in Nigeria. The company specializes in the manufacture of beta-lactam antibiotic range of products. It is well reputed for quality and focus in the industry. Our partnership is to leverage on the skills already acquired by Miraflash in her chosen area of specialization while we help her optimize her capacity by contract-manufacturing our beta-lactam products in Miraflash factory instead of importing same or duplicating efforts in same area of focus.
