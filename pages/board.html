Board of Directors

Dr. Olayiwola Gbola
Chairman
Dr Gbola Olayiwola is a Senior Lecturer and Researcher in the Faculty of Pharmacy, Obafemi Awolowo University. He is a pharmacist with Masters Degree in Business Administration and PhD in Pharmacology. In his over 30 years experience in teaching and research covering the various sectors of pharmaceutical and medical practice, he has been able to pass knowledge down to many generations of students, published many research papers aimed at proffering solutions to business and professional challenges in the Nigerian Pharmaceutical Industry. His dynamic disposition to a balanced synergy of knowledge with practice is expected to add value to the Board of Synergy Specialty Ltd especially in the area of development of customer- centric products and services and future research based innovations.
