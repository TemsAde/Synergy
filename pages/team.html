Managing Director
Ademola Adeoti
Ademola Adeoti is a 1991 graduate of Pharmacy from Obafemi Awolowo University, Ile- Ife. He has working experience of over twenty years in various pharmaceutical companies/ establishments. Among which are: Federal Ministry of Health & Social Service, Lagos State Hospital Management Board, Radiant Health pharmacy etc. He was the pioneer Pharmacist of Fidson Healthcare Plc in 1995, led the company’s marketing team until 1999 when he became the General Manager of the company. He became a Board member (Executive Director) of the company in year 2000 and he retired as Operations Director in December 2004 to form the Synergy Group of companies. (A multi-faceted manufacturing, sales and marketing company in the health sector of the economy.
